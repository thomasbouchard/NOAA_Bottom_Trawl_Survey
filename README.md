# Analyzing 58 years of Northeast Fisheries Bottom Trawl Survey Data (1968-2025)

The NOAA Northeast Fisheries Science Centers' bottom trawl survey is one of the longest-running surveys of its kind.  This survey contains 58 years of data exploring the distribution, abundance, and biodiversity of groundfish and invertebrates on the continental shelf between February and April. Over 1 million samples and 100 features make this dataset great for practicing skills learned in the BU Data Science Curriculum.  

This dataset is an exceptional real-world caset study for 
- Advanced data cleaning
- Merging multi-table relational structures
- Predictive Modeling
- Geospatial visualization
- Time-series analysis

This is a portfolio piece to demonstrate competance in cleaning, exploratory data analysis, modeling, and visualizations.
